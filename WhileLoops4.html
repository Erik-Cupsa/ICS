Pig Latin

By Erik Cupsa

This program will turn any sentence you input into pig latin.
In this language, words that begin with a vowel have 'ay' added at the end. Words that begin with a consonant have the consonant moved to the end and 'ay' is added at the end.
When you are ready to start, input your sentence in the textbox below and click the start button.
